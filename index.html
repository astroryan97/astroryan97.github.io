The Platform

My platform for this year’s annual erection is one meter high and composed of wood. As with all platforms I stand on it, kneel on it, and sometimes I sit on it but eventually I step off the platform.

Education

With the education of students being THE number one priority, I'm committed to eliminating waste and providing quality education to all students.

  • Putting all our grades up for adoption so we don’t have to raise them ourselves.
  • Provide higher education by making our buildings taller.
  • Replace Professors on leave with pictures of famous scientists.
  • Add spelling to the edumacational kricklum.
  • Add illiteracy as a university major.

Finanace

With student finances being THE number one priority

  • Putting all student’s debt on Visa.
  • Selling the engineering building at an antique auction in California.
  • Make the university tuition free by allowing advertising in lecture halls.

Health

With the health of the students being THE number one priority, I promise that all students will get their money’s worth when it comes to healthcare.

  • Handing out free condoms to protect students from their midterm and final exams.
  • Responding to the demand of the students, traffic will be reduced in the Wellness Centre by eliminating the waiting area.
  • Reduce the number of accidents by wrapping all workers in bubble wrap.

Univeristy Pride

  • Make the university’s theme song “All alone” by Blind Witness since it perfectly captures the university lifestyle.
  • Repeal the law of reflection so our failures don’t come back to haunt us.
  • Reclaim MacHall from the university by planting a flag there.
  • Add one holiday per month. April 1st and my birthday will also become holidays, and it will be forbidden to teach the day after a holiday.

Justice

With justice being THE number one priority, I will take the necessary steps to ensure justice for all students.

Environment

With the environment being THE number one priority

  • Ban Canadian winters on campus.
  • Scientists predict that in the next 20 years, global warming will threaten the existence of human beings. We can do better! I promise to make it happen in 10 years!
  • In order to fight global warming, we will force all buildings to leave their windows open in the summer and to operate the air conditioning to the maximum.<\li>
  • Make the buildings on campus greener: forest green, pale green, khaki green, and neon green.
  • Abolish the environment because it's too hard to keep clean and it takes up too much space.

Public Security

With student security being THE number one priority, I'm committed to making the necessary efforts to ensure the safety of all students.

  • Count all our buildings to make sure Mount Royal College didn’t steal any.
  • Ban Donald Trump from the university campus.
  • Equip Safewalk employees with shotguns and tasers.

Infrastructure

With the infrastructure being THE number one priority

  • Put at least one Tim Hortons in every building on campus.
  • Build a bridge from TFDL to MacHall.
  • Attach airbags to campus computers to prevent them from crashing.<\li>
  • Make all the hallways one way only.
